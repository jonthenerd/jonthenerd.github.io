The batteries on the IP30 and CN3 are fully charged and ready for some tinkering. Upgrading the Firmware from 3.01 to 3.16 I looked into upgrading the firmware on the IP30 and it looks to require a MiniSD card. MiniSD is the never-mentioned step brother of SD and MicroSD… meaning stores don’t carry it anymore. I’ve got a MiniSD card and reader on order from Amazon… so updating the IP30 will have to wait.
